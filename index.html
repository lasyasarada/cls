About Me

                                   
               

Hi, I'm Lasya — a passionate Software Engineer currently working at Telstra, with 4 years of experience in the IT industry. I started my career at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), where I spent over 3.5 years as a BMC Remedy Developer and Administrator. My core expertise is in configuring and optimizing ITSM applications like Incident, Change, Problem, Release, and Work Order Management. I've developed and customized workflows, approval mappings, and SLAs to meet business needs. I'm also skilled in using Data Management Tools (DMT) and handling system integrations efficiently. Enhancing user experience through customized forms and views in Remedy has been a key part of my work. I’ve been recognized with awards like “Star of the Month” and “On the Spot Award” for my dedication and performance. I’m known for being analytical, quality-focused, and always eager to learn and grow.

Outside of work, I love drawing, watching movies, writing blogs on Instagram, and exploring spirituality. I live by the motto: “Live and let live,” and believe in the power of continuous learning — “Learn, unlearn, and relearn.” I strive to live a conscious and inspired life every day.
